Structured plans

Build your own 8–16 week program or start from a template. 2–5 sessions a week, with weight & rest guidance baked in.

Progress tracking

Tonnage, attendance, PRs and per-cycle summaries. Your history is resilient even when you change the plan.

Charts that show the long game: volume trends, weekly digests and the metrics that actually move the needle.

Strava sync

Pull in your runs and rides — heart-rate zones, pace and elevation right alongside your lifting.
