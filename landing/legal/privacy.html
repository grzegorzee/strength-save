2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account data: email address, display name, optional profile photo, password (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider), language and unit preferences. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email (or Apple's private relay address) from that provider.
  • Training data: workout plans, exercises, sets, repetitions, weights, rest times, workout duration, training notes, skipped exercises, and self-reported effort metrics (RPE), discomfort/pain ratings and set quality ratings.
  • Body measurements: body weight and circumference measurements you choose to log.
  • AI Coach conversations: messages you send to the in-app AI assistant and the training context needed to answer them.
  • Support correspondence: messages you send us by email.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data: app version, device type, operating system, language, crash and error information, and basic in-app usage events (e.g. feature usage counters) used to operate and improve the Service.
  • Push notification tokens, if you enable notifications.

Information from optional integrations (only if you connect them)

  • Strava: with your authorization we import your Strava activities (type, distance, time, pace, heart-rate data, elevation, calories) to show analytics in the app. You can disconnect Strava at any time in the app, which stops further imports.
  • Apple Health / HealthKit and Apple Watch: if you grant permission, the watch app reads heart-rate data during a workout session and writes your workout sessions to Apple Health. HealthKit data is used solely to provide workout features, is never used for advertising or marketing, and is never disclosed to third parties for their own purposes.

You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting the data, disconnecting the integration, or deleting your account; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
